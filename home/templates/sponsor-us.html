Special Sponsorship

Awards Ceremony $2000

PyCon Africa will be holding the very first Pan-African Python Community Service Awards. Awards will be given to community members who have played an active role in supporting the growth of the Python community in Africa. Your sponsorship will go towards:

  • the award itself, each winner will receive a plaque that will include an inscription of your company logo.
  • a small cash prize for the winner that will be used to seed back into their local community.
  • Catering and decor for the Award Ceremony.
  • Your company logo on the PyCon Africa website and on slides throughout the conference.

Speakers Dinner $1000

All the speakers attending the conference will be invited to attend a private speakers dinner. The event is an opportunity for speakers from across the continent and the world to meet and network.

Your company will be acknowledged throughout the event and we invite you to bring banners to be placed in the dining hall. Your company logo will also be on the PyCon Africa website.

Swag Bags $700

All of our conference attendees will be given Swag Bags full of mementos and tools from the conference. Sponsorship of the conference Swag Bags includes your company branding on all of the Bags. Your company Logo on the PyCon Africa website and on slides throughout the conference.

Coffee $500

No developer conference would be complete without coffee! This year we have opened up the opportunity for a company to fuel up our attendees by being our official coffee sponsor. Your logo will be placed at all coffee fueling stations and will be placed on our website for acknowledgment.
