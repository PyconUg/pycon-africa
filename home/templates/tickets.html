Tickets



How much should I pay?

Our tickets for Pycon Africa 2020 follow a ‘pay what you can’ model. However, we would like to encourage you, to support our event and community as best you can.

Student ticket: $USD20
If you are using Python as a hobby, as a freelancer or student the personal ticket is for you.

Personal ticket: $USD50
If you are using Python as a hobby, as a freelancer or student the personal ticket is for you.

Business ticket: $USD100
If a company is paying for your ticket, please pay for a business ticket.


Donations

If you would like to support Pycon Africa, please consider donating any amount you can. You are also welcome to reach out to our team if you would like to become a special sponsor or support the conference.

Where your support goes:
Any extra proceeds from the conference will go into our grants budget, which we will use to fund financial aid for the next edition of Pycon Africa. This allows us to make the conference as accessible as possible to a wide range of Pythonista from across the continent and the world.

Terms and Questions
If you have questions about our tickets, the process, etc. please contact us using team@pycon.africa
