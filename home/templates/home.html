PyCon Africa 2020 will be Online

PyCon Africa is the gathering of the Pan African Python Community.

We had the very first edition in 2019 and while the conference cannot take place in person as planned this year our speakers, presenters, and sponsors will be providing recordings of what they were preparing for PyCon Africa 2020 to share with the community online.

2019 | Highlights

PyCon Africa 2019 was the first-ever pan-African meeting of the Python programming community, and took place in Accra, Ghana from the 6th - 10th August. It included five days of workshops, talks, sprints and events such as a Django Girls tutorial.
