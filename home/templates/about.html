About Us



The | Team

The organising team of PyCon Africa 2020 includes experienced Python community conference organisers from Africa and Europe.
Between them they have run multiple international conferences, including PyCons in Nigeria, Ghana, Zimbabwe, Namibia, United Kingdom as well as several editions of DjangoCon Europe.

team@pycon.africa

The | City

Accra is a friendly and safe city. It's a hub for the west African software industry with excellent facilities and international connections.
The vibrant Ghanaian Python community has already hosted PyCon Ghana in 2018, PyCon Africa 2019 and is looking forward to welcoming Pythonistas from all over Africa and the world for the second time.
